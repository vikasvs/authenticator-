We created this platform during the COVID-19 pandemic, where we noticed many of our friends having their internships rescinded and losing their jobs. We thought with so many qualified individuals who never got an opportunity to show what they could bring to the table at a company, how could they leverage that offer into finding future opportunities. Hence we created this platform, where recruiters/managers can validate prospective employees offer letters, and provide recommendations on their behalf, giving them great future opportunities.
